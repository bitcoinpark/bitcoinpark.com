Bitcoin Custody & Treasury Summit

A curated, two-day gathering for institutional and corporate treasurers, custody providers, and Bitcoin infrastructure builders.

September 17th & 18th

Purpose and Mission

Join us for a curated, two-day gathering focused on institutional-grade Bitcoin custody, treasury management, and the future of digital asset infrastructure.

The Bitcoin Custody & Treasury Summit brings together the world's leading institutional and corporate treasurers, Bitcoin-native and traditional asset managers, custody providers, and infrastructure builders for focused discussions on security, resilience, and institutional-grade Bitcoin adoption.

Themes & Topics

Explore the critical topics shaping the future of institutional Bitcoin custody and treasury management.

Cold vs Warm vs MPC Custody

Deep dive into the technical and operational differences between custody approaches, their security trade-offs, and implementation strategies for institutional environments.

Collaborative Custody

Explore emerging models of shared custody arrangements, multi-signature setups, and distributed trust mechanisms for institutional Bitcoin holdings.

BTC on Corporate Balance Sheets

Strategic considerations for corporate Bitcoin allocation, accounting treatment, regulatory compliance, and integration with existing treasury operations.

Estate Planning & Succession

Critical discussions on Bitcoin inheritance, key management, and ensuring long-term access to digital assets across generations and organizational changes.

Internal Controls & Governance

Best practices for establishing robust internal controls, audit procedures, and governance frameworks for institutional Bitcoin custody operations.

Regulatory Dialogue

Engage with regulators and policymakers on the evolving landscape of digital asset custody regulations and compliance requirements.
