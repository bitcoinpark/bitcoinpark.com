Summit Overview

Bitcoin Takeover is a curated gathering of approximately 200 entrepreneurs, developers, policymakers, and capital allocators who are building the infrastructure for sovereign payments and sound money. United by a mission to restore genuine technological discourse at SXSW, we are reclaiming Austin's creative and innovative spirit.

1
Day One
Power of Payments

Dedicated to the infrastructure and innovation enabling Bitcoin as a medium of exchange. The day examines the technical, economic, and practical realities of building payment systems on the Bitcoin and Lightning Network. Sessions cover payment processor architecture, merchant adoption strategies, custody solutions for payment flows, cross-border settlement, point-of-sale integration, and the convergence of Bitcoin payments with emerging technologies.

2
Day Two
Proof of Work

A showcase of eight founders and builders demonstrating proof of work through real businesses and projects. Each builder shares their vision, projects, and strategic insights through presentations offering tangible examples of real businesses supporting the Bitcoin industry. Categories span custody, infrastructure, open source development, AI integration, education, energy, and mining.
