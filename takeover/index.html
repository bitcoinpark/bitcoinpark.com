TAKEOVER SUMMIT

2026

The Bitcoin Park Takeover Summit provides an opportunity for the global community of Bitcoiners to encounter cutting-edge ideas, discover new interests, and network with other professionals who share a similar appetite for forward-focused experiences in payments and proof of work.

2026 Schedule

Summit Overview

Two days of deep dives into Bitcoin payments and proof of work infrastructure

1
Day One
Payment Summit

Join us for a comprehensive exploration of Bitcoin payment infrastructure, Lightning Network innovations, and the future of digital payments. Day one focuses on how Bitcoin is revolutionizing the way we transact, from micro-payments to enterprise solutions.

Topics include Lightning Network development, payment protocols, merchant adoption, and the technical foundations that make Bitcoin the superior payment system.

2
Day Two
Proof of Work

Dive deep into Bitcoin's security model and the critical importance of proof of work. Day two examines mining infrastructure, energy considerations, network security, and the economic incentives that secure the Bitcoin network.

Explore topics including mining operations, hash rate trends, energy dynamics, and how proof of work creates the most secure monetary network in history.
