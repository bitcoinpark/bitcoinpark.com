About the Forum

The Case for Bitcoin in Healthcare is a curated gathering of physicians, hospital administrators, healthcare entrepreneurs, capital allocators, and policymakers who are shaping the future of health and sound money. United by a shared mission, we are pioneering new models for how healthcare is delivered, financed, and incentivized in the age of Bitcoin and beyond.

Hosted in the spirit of Bitcoin Park experiences, this gathering centers on the transformative theme of sound health through sound money, and how Bitcoin can address the systemic inefficiencies of fiat-driven healthcare.

Core Theme

The Vital Signs of Sound Money

Just as physicians monitor vital signs to assess patient health, Bitcoin provides the vital signs of a healthy monetary system: transparency, predictability, and stability. From direct primary care and self-funded insurance models to Bitcoin-denominated HSAs and longevity-focused care, Bitcoin stands as both a unit of account and a cornerstone of health sovereignty.

Diagnose

Identify the root causes of fiat-driven dysfunction in healthcare economics, from opaque pricing to misaligned incentives and unsustainable costs.

Build relationships across medicine, finance, and policy. Meet the practitioners and innovators leading the shift to Bitcoin-native healthcare models.

Heal

Take home actionable frameworks for integrating Bitcoin into your practice, personal finances, and patient care, starting today.

  • 1:05pm – 1:20pm
    The US Healthcare Mess, and How Bitcoin Revitalizes the Industry Keynote

    Stuart Lackey

    SOUND HSA

    Stuart Lackey is the Co-Founder and CEO of SOUND HSA. Stuart has played critical leadership roles in driving early-stage market adoption and revenue growth for surgical device and med-tech companies, two of which went public (SI-Bone and TranS1). He's also worked as a strategic growth consultant for multiple healthcare companies. He first learned of bitcoin in 2016 prompting him to co–found Solaster Health, which sought to create a decentralized, personal health data platform. Stuart completed his master's degree in Healthcare Informatics in 2018, which led to his vision for SOUND HSA and wider bitcoin adoption across the US healthcare market.

  • 1:20pm – 2:05pm
    Building the Bitcoin Health Economy: Payments, Treasury Management, and Circular Healthcare Systems Panel
    • Mark Connors — Riskdimensions
    • Dr. Robert Rahimi — Baylor Scott & White, BPN
    • Dr. Mark Link — Link Dental

    This comprehensive panel examines how Bitcoin infrastructure can fundamentally reshape healthcare finance through three interconnected frameworks. Panelists explore the mechanics of implementing Bitcoin-based payment systems for medical services, replacing traditional payment rails with peer-to-peer transactions that reduce intermediary costs and settlement delays. Panelists address treasury management strategies for healthcare businesses adopting Bitcoin reserves, examining volatility mitigation, regulatory compliance, and integration with existing financial operations. And finally, speakers investigate circular health economy concepts where Bitcoin facilitates closed-loop value exchange within healthcare ecosystems, enabling direct provider-patient relationships, transparent pricing, and programmable health savings accounts. Drawing on real-world implementation challenges and opportunities, panelists provide actionable frameworks for healthcare organizations considering Bitcoin adoption.

  • 2:05pm – 2:50pm
    Root Causes of Fiat Health Problems: Perverse Incentives and Bitcoin Solutions Panel
    • Abel James Bascom — The Wild Diet
    • Dr. Mark Stephany — Bitcoin Physicians Network
    • Dr. Daniel Ostermayer — Bitcoin Physicians Network
    • Dr. Ahmad Ammous — Harvard, Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center

    Dissect the fundamental structural problems plaguing modern healthcare systems, tracing how fiat currency inflation and centralized financial control have created misaligned incentives throughout the healthcare value chain. Panelists examine how fee-for-service models incentivize volume over outcomes, how third-party payment systems disconnect consumers from true costs, and how centralized intermediaries extract value while obscuring pricing transparency. The discussion explores metabolic health dysfunction as a symptom of these systemic failures, analyzing how ultra-processed foods and pharmaceutical dependencies have become profit centers rather than health solutions. Panelists present Bitcoin as a catalyst for restructuring healthcare economics, enabling sovereign health, equitable resource distribution, transparent pricing mechanisms, and restoring physician autonomy while reducing administrative burden and intermediary rent-seeking.

  • 2:50pm – 3:15pm
    Break
  • 3:15pm – 4:00pm
    Hard Money Health Business Models: HSAs, Direct Primary Care, and Decentralized Health Finance Panel
    • Jon Gordon — SOUND HSA
    • Dr. Geetinder Goyal — First Primary Care
    • Andy Schoonover — CrowdHealth

    This panel explores the cutting edge of healthcare financing mechanisms enabled by Bitcoin technology, examining how it revolutionizes health savings accounts, direct primary care arrangements, and alternative insurance models. Panelists discuss Bitcoin-denominated health savings accounts that protect purchasing power against inflation while enabling borderless portability and programmable compliance. The conversation addresses Direct Primary Care integration with Bitcoin payments, creating transparent subscription models that bypass insurance overhead and restore patient-physician relationships. Speakers evaluate peer-to-peer health risk-sharing networks, crowdfunding for medical expenses, and decentralized health finance applications built on Bitcoin infrastructure. Drawing from regulatory landscapes, implementation case studies, and actuarial considerations, this session provides practical roadmaps for organizations and individuals seeking to leverage Bitcoin for accessible, affordable, and autonomous healthcare financing.

  • 4:00pm – 4:15pm
    The Future of Healthcare Finance: Unlocking New Opportunities in a $5T Industry Keynote

    Dr. Rich Lassiter

    Bitcoin Physicians Network

    Dr. Rich Lassiter, emergency medicine physician and prominent Bitcoin educator, delivers our closing keynote, exploring Bitcoin's potential to unlock emergent opportunities within the $5 trillion US healthcare industry. Drawing from his dual expertise as a practicing emergency room physician and bitcoin advocate, Dr. Lassiter examines how Bitcoin can address healthcare's most pressing financial challenges: runaway costs, lack of price transparency, insurance complexity, and limited patient financial autonomy. He presents real-world scenarios for Bitcoin adoption across healthcare stakeholders, from hospital treasury strategies and physician payment systems to patient savings mechanisms and medical tourism. The keynote explores new investment models as Dr. Lassiter challenges attendees to envision a future where Bitcoin serves as foundational infrastructure for accessible, efficient, and patient-centered healthcare finance.
