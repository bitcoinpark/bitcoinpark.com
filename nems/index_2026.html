About the Summit

The Nashville Energy and Mining Summit 2026 is a curated gathering of approximately 200 operators, engineers, policymakers, entrepreneurs, capital allocators, and institutional leaders who are shaping the future of energy, grids, and compute. United by a shared mission, we are pioneering new models for how power is generated, distributed, and monetized in the age of Bitcoin and beyond.

Programming Tracks

Explore the critical topics shaping the future of energy and mining.

Generation: Powering the Future

Exploring innovations in energy production, from natural gas and renewables to nuclear and SMRs. This track highlights how diverse sources of generation can be harnessed, monetized, and integrated into a more resilient energy landscape that supports both local communities and global markets.

Transmission: Building the Modern Grid

Focusing on the evolving role of transmission operators, utilities, and engineers in optimizing grid dynamics. Sessions cover grid flexibility, stability, and new business models that emerge when power is treated not only as a utility but also as an asset.

Load: Bitcoin, AI, and Beyond

Dedicated to demand-side innovation, this track covers Bitcoin mining, AI, HPC, and other compute-intensive industries as flexible loads that stabilize grids, create economic opportunities, and open new pathways for monetizing the megawatt.

Markets & Monetization

Zooming in on the economics of energy and compute. This includes energy trading, capital allocation, treasury strategy, and the mechanisms by which megawatts become monetized, from local power purchase agreements to global-scale markets.

Policy, Security & Governance

Addressing the governmental frameworks that shape the industry. Policymakers, regulators, legal experts, and security specialists will discuss enabling policies, grid sovereignty, energy independence, and how governance models can unlock domestic and international flourishing.

The State of the Art

Exploring the technological innovations that enable Bitcoin mining and AI superclusters to happen all over the world. Connecting with the technical expertise as well as the operators experimenting and proving out these technologies in the field.
