# Imagine IF — Complete Visual Identity Reference

Source: Imagine IF Brandsheet 2025 (designed by Leah Dorrian / dorriandesigns, July 2025)

---

## Brand Philosophy

Imagine IF was born from a belief that we need more spaces where people are encouraged to imagine big, wild, positive futures — not just in theory, but in practice. It's a call to action for dreamers, builders, and believers to come together and shape the world we want to live in. Each "Imagine IF..." talk plants a seed for what's possible when we combine bold imagination with human action.

The brand personality is imaginative, bold, hopeful, human-centered, and visionary, reflecting a core belief in a brighter future empowered by innovation. It champions optimism about the future and empowerment through technology, leveraging creativity as a catalyst for change.

Values underscore freedom and individual agency, fostering community-driven innovation. The brand voice is consistently uplifting and inspiring, designed to be both thought-provoking yet accessible — speaking with confidence, grounded in possibility, maintaining a conversational, emotionally resonant, and future-oriented tone that invites engagement and inspires action.

### Differentiator

Imagine IF stands apart by rooting its vision in Freedom Tech (bitcoin) ethos + human flourishing + creative optimism. It's not about elite access or academic theory — it's about real people using tools to build a better world. The "Imagine IF..." format is emotionally engaging and personal, giving it a distinct voice that combines imagination with agency.

## Logo System

### Primary Logo (Stacked)
- Custom brandmark (abstract sun ascending behind a mountain peak with upward arrow) + "IMAGINE IF" wordmark stacked below
- Centered around the theme of "exploration"
- The mountain view embodies an expansive perspective, suggesting the ability to perceive beyond conventional limits
- The accompanying arrow points upward, denoting upward thinking or progress
- Use this variation most frequently when space allows

### Secondary Logo (Horizontal)
- Same elements arranged horizontally — brandmark beside wordmark
- Use when vertical layout is restricted by space or composition

### Brandmark (Icon Only)
- The abstract sun/mountain/arrow symbol alone
- For: social media profiles, watermarks, favicons, website footers

### Wordmark (Logotype Only)
- "IMAGINE IF" text only
- Wordmark typeface: **Encode Sans Expanded Bold**
- For: when a typographic-only treatment is preferred

### Logo Usage
- Primary usage is white logo on dark (royal purple/midnight blue) backgrounds
- Can also be used in brand colors on light backgrounds
- Follow standard logo don'ts: do not stretch, rotate, recolor outside approved palette, or place on busy backgrounds

## Typography

### Primary Headline Font: Rethink Sans Bold
Used for all headlines and high-impact text. A clean, modern sans-serif that conveys confidence and forward-thinking energy.

| Weight | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Bold | All headlines and section titles |

### Body Font: Rethink Sans Regular
Used for paragraphs and supporting body text. Maintains the same font family for visual cohesion.

| Weight | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Regular | All body text and paragraphs |

### Accent Font: Cormorant Garamond Bold Italic
Reserved exclusively for the "Imagine IF" phrase when used in hero moments. This serif italic creates an elegant, aspirational quality that distinguishes the brand name in feature placements.

| Weight | Use |
|--------|-----|
| Bold Italic | "Imagine IF" phrase in hero moments only |

### Wordmark Typeface: Encode Sans Expanded Bold
Used exclusively in the logo wordmark — do not use for body copy or headlines.

## Imagery & Icons

### Visual Aesthetic
The brand embodies a futuristic yet warm aesthetic, combining clean, minimalist design with moments of imaginative flair. It aims for high event production quality that holds a grassroots, human tone.

### Illustration Approach
- Geometric, abstract illustrations using brand palette
- Warm gradients (orange-to-purple spectrum)
- Clean, minimalist compositions with moments of creative flair
- Abstract shapes (circles, arcs, lines) suggesting exploration and possibility
- Color blocking with primary palette colors

### Icon Style
- Line-weight icons in Midnight Blue or Royal Purple
- Clean, geometric, minimalist
- Categories include: globe/world, growth/nature, calendar/events, community/people, direction/navigation, grid/structure, media/imagery

### Photography
- High-quality photos of real events, community, and speakers
- Dark, atmospheric event photography with brand color lighting
- Pair with brand graphics and overlays

## Relationship to Bitcoin Park

Imagine IF is a distinct event series / initiative under the Bitcoin Park umbrella. It shares thematic DNA — Freedom Tech ethos, human flourishing, community — but has its own visual identity, color palette, and typography system. Do not blend Imagine IF's palette with Bitcoin Park's or AI Freedom Lab's palettes.

Key distinctions:
- **Imagine IF** uses purples and warm oranges (Sunrise Orange #e68158 vs Park Orange #FF9974 — related but different hues)
- **Imagine IF** uses Rethink Sans + Cormorant Garamond (vs BP's Optima + Source Sans Pro)
- **Imagine IF** imagery is more abstract/geometric with warm gradients (vs BP's flat vector illustrations)
