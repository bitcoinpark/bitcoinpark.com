The Vision

Welcome to the high-conviction summit for dreamers and doers; those shaping what's possible in Bitcoin, AI, energy, and freedom tech.
Join 500 of the most curious and capable minds for an intimate experience that blends the narrative power of human storytelling with the serendipity of summer camp.

The Format

Human centered keynotes begin with one question: "Imagine IF..."
Candid fireside chats with visionaries turning ideas into infrastructure.
Natural collisions with investors, engineers, policymakers, and entrepreneurs.
Three distinct programming stages: the Imagine IF theatre stage; a stage co-curated by Ten31 and OpenSats; and a third stage led by Bitcoin Park and 256 Foundation.

Every moment entertains, informs, and inspires.
The future isn't inherited.
We imagine, then we build.

Why Nashville?

Nashville is a city of rebirth, grit and creativity. The Athens of the South is where ideas stretch out and sing.
The Fisher Center, a European style opera house, encapsulates big visions and unforgettable moments mere steps from Bitcoin Park.
