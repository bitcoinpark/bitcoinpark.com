Laboratory Texas

The Texas Energy & Mining Summit 2026 is a curated gathering of operators, engineers, policymakers, entrepreneurs, capital allocators, and institutional leaders who are building at the intersection of energy, compute, and capital in the most dynamic energy market in North America. We explore how Texas has become the laboratory where Silicon Valley tech innovation meets East Coast financial engineering.

The Catalyst

Where deregulation meets entrepreneurship, where traditional ranchers meet Bitcoin miners, where grid physics meets market mechanics—the collision itself creates energy and possibility.

Market Structure

ERCOT's unique deregulated structure creates opportunities unavailable elsewhere, enabling rapid innovation and deployment through price signals and grid optimization.

Capital Formation

West Coast technology capital and East Coast financial sophistication meet in Texas, with the Texas Stock Exchange opening and novel financing structures driving infrastructure growth.

Community Integration

Traditional energy producers, Bitcoin miners, ranchers, and technology innovators collaborate in unprecedented ways to build resilient infrastructure.

Programming Themes

Full Stack Texas: Six themes exploring infrastructure, markets, and innovation

The Texas Electric Market: ERCOT and Beyond

Examining the unique characteristics of Texas's deregulated energy market and how ERCOT's structure creates opportunities unavailable in other states. Grid dynamics, price signals, and mechanisms enabling rapid innovation.

Fast Permitting and Development: The Texas Advantage

How Texas's regulatory environment enables faster project deployment than anywhere else. Permitting pathways, land development, interconnection processes, and community partnerships.

The Convergence: Bitcoin, AI, and Next-Gen Compute

The intersection of Bitcoin mining and AI workloads in Texas. Co-location strategies, grid economics optimization, infrastructure requirements, and the future of compute density.

Capital Formation and Financial Innovation

The financial engines driving Texas's energy transformation. The Texas Stock Exchange, novel financing structures, treasury strategies, and how Wall Street capital flows into Texas infrastructure.

Community Integration: Ranchers, Operators, and Local Leaders

Voices of landowners, ranchers, and operators integrating Bitcoin mining and energy projects. How traditional Texas industries are evolving and communities benefit from energy monetization.

Policy, Energy Security, and the International Bridge

Governmental frameworks and international dimensions shaping Texas's energy future. Grid sovereignty, energy independence, cross-border opportunities, and enabling innovation while maintaining reliability.
