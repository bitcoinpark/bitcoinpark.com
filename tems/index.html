About the Summit

The Texas Energy and Mining Summit 2026 is a curated gathering of approximately 200 operators, engineers, policymakers, entrepreneurs, capital allocators, and institutional leaders who are building at the intersection of energy, compute, and capital in the most dynamic energy market in North America. We explore how Texas has become the laboratory where Silicon Valley tech innovation meets East Coast financial engineering.

This gathering centers on the idea of the Energy Frontier in Laboratory Texas, and how the state's unique market structure, permitting environment, and entrepreneurial culture are redefining what's possible in energy monetization. From ERCOT's deregulated market design to the convergence of Bitcoin mining, AI, and next-generation power production, the summit explores how Texas is pioneering new models for grid optimization, resource allocation, and community resilience.

The Energy Frontier

Texas has emerged as the world's most dynamic laboratory for energy innovation, where deregulated markets, fast permitting, and entrepreneurial culture create unprecedented opportunities.

Laboratory Texas

Where Silicon Valley tech innovation meets East Coast financial engineering. Texas is the proving ground for the convergence of power generation, grid optimization, and Bitcoin mining.

Community Resilience

Traditional energy producers, Bitcoin miners, ranchers, and technology innovators are collaborating in unprecedented ways to build a more resilient energy economy.

Global Blueprint

As both proving ground and blueprint, Texas stands at the forefront of energy's future, offering lessons for energy markets worldwide.

Programming Tracks

Six tracks exploring the future of energy and mining in Texas

The Texas Electric Market

Examining ERCOT's unique deregulated structure and how Texas's market-driven approach creates opportunities unavailable elsewhere. Grid dynamics, price signals, and mechanisms enabling rapid innovation.

Fast Permitting & Development

How Texas's regulatory environment enables faster project deployment than anywhere else. Permitting pathways, land development, interconnection processes, and community partnerships.

The Convergence: Bitcoin, AI & Compute

The intersection of Bitcoin mining and AI workloads in Texas. Co-location strategies, grid economics optimization, infrastructure requirements, and the future of compute density.

Capital Formation & Financial Innovation

The financial engines driving Texas's energy transformation. The Texas Stock Exchange, novel financing structures, treasury strategies, and how Wall Street capital is flowing into Texas infrastructure.

Community Integration

Voices of landowners, ranchers, and operators integrating Bitcoin mining and energy projects. How traditional Texas industries are evolving and communities are benefiting from energy monetization.

Policy & Energy Security

Governmental frameworks and international dimensions shaping Texas's energy future. Grid sovereignty, energy independence, cross-border opportunities, and enabling innovation while maintaining reliability.
