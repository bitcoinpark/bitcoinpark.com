What is Grassroots?

Bitcoin Park invites meetup organizers to join us in Nashville, TN for a 2-day gathering focused on community collaboration and creating powerful meetup experiences.

This event is invite-only and limited to organizers.

Connect

Meet fellow Bitcoin meetup organizers from across the U.S. and world. Share what works and learn from each other's experiences.

Grow

Learn best practices for building thriving local Bitcoin communities. Take home actionable strategies for your meetup.

Collaborate

Build lasting relationships and create a network of organizers working together to strengthen Bitcoin adoption from the ground up.

Who is Invited?

Grassroots Bitcoin is for Bitcoin meetup organizers across the world. If you run a local BitDevs, Bitcoin meetup, or facilitate grassroots bitcoin education in your local community, we invite you to apply.

Meetup Organizers

Leaders who run local Bitcoin meetups and bring their communities together around sound money.

BitDevs Organizers

Technical community leaders who host Socratic seminars and developer-focused Bitcoin discussions.

Bitcoin Educators

Those running grassroots Bitcoin education programs in their local communities and beyond.
