Fedi Meetup Kit

Hero Image

Become part of a global Bitcoin community dedicated to privacy and freedom.

From circular economies in Africa to multi-meetup coalitions in Asia, Fedi is becoming an operating system for global freedom. Bitcoin Park is bringing those unique learnings to our community here in North America.

If you are passionate about the intersection between technology and freedom, this is how you can help others see why this matters — global thinking with local impact.

As a meetup leader, you can have access to:

  • The newly launched Fedi Meetup Kit Grant Program
  • Educational material that has been well-tested in the field
  • Global learnings about real-world Bitcoin adoption
  • Additional support from the Fedi and Bitcoin Park teams

If this is of interest to you, check out the educational material, grant program, meetup kit requests, and more. For all other questions, email fediorder@fedi.xyz.

Grow Your Community, One Meetup at a Time

Communities don't just grow online. They spark in rooms filled with conversation, shared pizza, and people learning together. Fedi and Bitcoin Park believe the future of freedom tech is built face-to-face through meetups, workshops, and local gatherings.

That's why we created the Meetup Kit Grant — a pilot program to help reduce the cost of hosting community events and make it easier to bring people together.

How It Works

Applications will be reviewed by the teams at Bitcoin Park and Fedi to select the participating meetups.

Start Small, Dream Big

We're currently accepting applications from communities in the US and Canada hosting events between April and May 2026. This is our pilot phase as we test and refine the program before expanding.

What You Get

  • Up to $500 USD to cover your meetup costs
  • Recognition and support as a Fedi community event
  • Connection to the Bitcoin Park network
  • Exclusivity and Access to Fedi VIP group
  • Ticket for the Imagine for the Meetup Coordinator ($5000 value)

Ready to bring your community together?

Applications are open for US & Canada.
