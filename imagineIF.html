The Vision

Imagine IF is not a tech conference. It’s a high-conviction summit for big-thinkers and bold-doers—those building the future in Bitcoin, AI, energy, and freedom tech.
We’re gathering ~500+ of the most curious and capable minds for a focused, intimate experience that blends the narrative power of TED with the serendipity of Allen & Co.

The Format

  • TED-style keynotes that start with one question: "Imagine IF..."
  • Candid fireside chats with visionaries turning ideas into infrastructure.
  • Natural collisions with investors, engineers, policymakers, and entrepreneurs.
  • Three distinct programming stages: the Imagine IF theatre stage; a stage co-curated by Ten31 and OpenSats; and a third stage led by Bitcoin Park and 256 Foundation.

Our aim is for every talk to entertain, inform, and inspire.
And every moment will remind us: the future isn’t inherited—it’s imagined, then built.

Why Nashville?

Nashville is a city of rebirth, creativity, and grit. It’s a place where ideas are allowed to stretch out and sing.
The Fisher Center, walking distance from Bitcoin Park, brings world-class acoustics and theatrical flair to the conversation—setting the tone for big visions and unforgettable moments.
